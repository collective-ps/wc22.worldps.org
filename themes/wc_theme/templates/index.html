Welcome to WC2022.

Originally started in 2008, the Pen Spinning World Cup represents a biennial competition where international pen spinning teams compete online to showcase their skills at the highest level.

This year's event continues tradition as the 7th event in the World Cup series, including a comprehensive prize pool from our sponsors -- the biggest prize pool featured so far.

A unique change to this year's registration, teams of 5 may be formed with members from any community, with an optional 6th member as an alternate.

Prize Pool

WC2022 features a $1000 USD prize pool.

1st place team - $500 USD

2nd place team - $250 USD

3rd place team - $125 USD

4th place team - $125 USD
